JED UX/UI Group Project 1

Creating an ecommerce mobile app from scratch. Focusing on users who support small businesses. When purchasing from ecommerce apps most of the time a percentage of the money goes to the company. With enough research there were many issues that users who shopped on ecommerce apps faced. JED was built to solve those issues.

Interview Questions


  • What usually drives you towards shopping from a small business?
  • Are there certain factors that play in to building trust with an unknown seller?
  • Overall when purchasing a product online, what is your process to gather information and inspiration?
  • Are there any benefits you have experienced from buying from a small business?
  • When you visit a small business online what content captures your attention?
  • Would it be helpful to have more transparency with the amount of money that is going to the creator vs the tool they are using?

User Insight

During this interview we discovered many online- shoppers struggle with long shipment from small businesses or unable to make an order to another state or country. Therefore, we believe that creating a reliable shopping app where customers can view verified information about small businesses, their products, and reviews will open up opportunities for small businesses to gain trust from customers, and thus, improve customer experience. We might do this by providing platform that unite all small businesses which gives them an excellent feature e.g.: fast free shipping not only locally but also international distances as well. It will help grow local business into international. Also, our users will be able to see transparently contribution and they will allow to do donations to their favorite brands.

Proto Persona

User Persona

Samantha is a 28-year-old single and free spirited individual in search of a reliable platform to shop online that will help support small and medium scale businesses with great and marketable products in the market that she can purchase thereby help grow these businesses.

Problem Statement

Online shoppers who want to support small businesses need a reliable platform that is built on trust and transparency because shoppers often have to spend a lot of time combing through reviews to verify the genuineness of the product and don't know how much of their money is actually going back to the seller. How might we provide a space for shoppers to shop stress-free while feeling confident that they are making a contribution to the small businesses they buy from.

Solution

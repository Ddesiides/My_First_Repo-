Problems

The Performing Animal Welfare Society is a non profit
organization that was created to provide sanctuary to
abused, abandoned, and retired captive wildlife. We
have observed that the information on our website
appears outdated and overwhelming. Which is causing
visitors to seek out other cause support instead.

Our Solution

We believe there is an opportunity to improve our
website (for both desktop and mobile) so that patrons
have a more successful experience leading to increase
donations

User Research

User Persona

We created a user persona based on the website
to get an idea of what type of users who would
donate to the Animal Welfare Society website

  • Ensure users are supporting legitimate organizations
  • Fast and easy donation process
  • Simple easy website to scroll through
  • Indicate website is trustworthy

    • Interview Questions

  • Does the homepage show the mission/goals of the organization
  • Does the header/navigation feel overwhelming and clear?
  • Are users able to easily navigate the “contact Us" portion of the site?
  • Can users easily find the “Adopt an Animal” landing page

    • We came up with questions for users to answer to get ideas of what needed improvement of the website. We got great feedback where all responses were put into a affinity diagram. On the affinity diagram all responses were put into groups of eight. We organized into demographics, about PAWs, navigation, internet use, contact info, UI observations, “Adopt an Animal”, and the website overall.

    Design

  • Brainstorm to breakdown the L2s and L3s of the naviagtion to keep it simple for the users
  • Created a UI style guide to keep the team all in the same design process
  • Sketched low fidelity to high fidelity wireframes and created a clickable prototype
  • When designing the landing page, decided to have a dropdown feature for the user to choose where they specifically wanted to donate towards

    • Results

    Working with a team there was a lot of problem solving. We had to completely start from scratch due to the outdated website. Testing of the high fidelity wireframes users understood the goal of the non profit website. There was less information but enough for the users to have knowledge. There was many ways to donate to the Performing Animal Welfare Society which was a bit confusing even to our team. My role was to focus on the donation page. I successfully accomplished a fast and easy donation page for our users.
